Company Launches

Terrakotta: Add voice as a channel to grow your business 🗣️

TL;DR: Terrakotta is a web-phone that uses personalized AI voicemails to double the number of contacts you can engage over the phone. Voicemails left using Terrakotta are personalized to every contact and generate more callbacks than generic scripts.

👋Hey Everyone! We’re Kus, Vincent, and Gabe (from left to right) and we are the founders of Terrakotta. Terrakotta lets users leverage their unique voice (yes, their literal voice) to grow their business. We’re calling it ‘voice nurture’.

Problem

Voicemail is a great growth channel to generate interest and callbacks from leads, but historically it hasn’t scaled well. Leaving voicemails during call sessions is:

  • Time-consuming
  • Repetitive
  • Tiring

Solution: ☎️⚡️Here’s what a call session looks like in Terrakotta⚡️☎️

With Terrakotta users can clone their voice, load up a contact list, and set a dynamic AI voicemail script that will be personalized for unanswered calls. From there, users can flip on the auto-dial feature and zoom through their call list, reaching up to 100 contacts/hour.

(Don’t worry: the contacts are not real people; the phone numbers are our many test lines)

About the squad

  • Vincent was an incoming Software Engineer at Palantir before dropping out of college to found Terrakotta.
  • Kus was a Senior Analyst at McKinsey where he wrote custom data solutions and advised principal investors / GTM execs on strategy.
  • Gabe was a software engineer for Lyft Business and Human Interest (S15).

We (strangely) have lots of friends in commercial real estate, and the number one thing they complain about is making outbound phone calls. We’re using our experience with software development and GTM in different contexts to take on this problem in commercial real estate and prove the ability of voice nurture as a growth channel.

Our asks

  • If you’re making lots of cold calls and want to do it more efficiently - try it out!: https://www.terrakotta.ai/
  • Get in touch with us if you’re looking for new channels like voicemail to grow your business
  • Give us feedback on what you think of the voice-cloning feature - how accurate do you think it is?
